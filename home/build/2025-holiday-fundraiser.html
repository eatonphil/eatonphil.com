2025 Holiday Fundraiser

Access to (and support for) education is incredibly important to me. So I'm celebrating this holiday season with two of my favorite things: chatting with you and fundraising for educational non-profits.

If you donate any amount over $10 and send me a receipt (screenshot is fine), I will list your name and a link of your choice (SFW, etc.) on this page.

If you donate any amount over $50 and send me a receipt (screenshot is fine), I will take a 30-minute call with you on any topic you'd like. Or I'll meet you in person in NYC when you're around.
