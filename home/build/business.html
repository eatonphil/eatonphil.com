My favorite business books

If you limit yourself to the Business section of a bookstore you're going to miss numerous impactful stories and lessons. This page collects the books that have taught me the most about business and processes and humans.

This list consists of books I read between 2016 and 2025. I omit books I read and gave less than a 3-star rating.
