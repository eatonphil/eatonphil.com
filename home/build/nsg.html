Northeast Systems Group on Discord

I host a Discord (120+ members) for folks in systems software in the Northeast US. So for folks at (or interested in working at) companies building stuff like compilers, databases, DNS, Linux hosting, devtools, etc. And focused geographically around DC, Philly, NYC, and Boston.

The big benefit of a group like this is to meet other folks in similar fields in the area and to find cool local jobs.
