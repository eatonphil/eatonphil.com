Favorite recipes

My basic requirement is that recipes don't require a lot of skill. They aren't all necessarily short though. Some require a lot of preparatory cutting unless you have a food processor (like latkes).

General notes

  • If a recipe that isn't supposed to be very garlic-y says to use a few cloves of garlic, always use a full head of garlic
  • If a recipe is supposed to be garlic-y, use multiple heads of garlic (at least two)
  • If a recipe calls for some amount of spinach or kale in a way that the greens get cooked and shrink, you can add 2-4 times the amount of greens and not taste a difference. You'll eat more greens. And greens in soup tend to hold soup flavor quite well.
