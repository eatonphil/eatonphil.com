Sundays Reading in the Park, NYC

One of my favorite things to do on a Sunday is to visit my favorite parks and read a book. And while I normally do this alone, I'd kind of rather do it with other people!

Fill out this Google Form if you'd like to get invites!

Who is this for?

If you love to read and you love parks, this is for you!

How will it work?

You bring a book, bring a coffee/tea/matÃ©.The quiet reading session will run from 11AM-11:45AM. After that it's unstructured social time.

I will send out a request for invites each week it runs. Like other events I run, the first 20 people to respond get a calendar invite! Please don't show up without a calendar invite!

How often?

I go almost every weekend to a park to read. And the weather isn't perfect year round. So it would be great to run this every other weekend, but that will depend on interest/turnout.

What prompted this?

Most of my network is software developers and I'd like to escape the bubble. I keep running events like the NYC Systems Coffee Club and NYC Systems, so I've been wanting to try something completely unrelated to career networking.
