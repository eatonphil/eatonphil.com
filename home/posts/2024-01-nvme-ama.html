title = Reddit AMA with Samsung NVMe experts ---

I am very excited that [Klaus Jensen](https://www.linkedin.com/in/klausbirkelund/) from Samsung, along with a few of his colleagues, have agreed to join [/r/databasedevelopment](https://www.reddit.com/r/databasedevelopment/) for an hour-long AMA on all things NVMe. To be held at the end of January.

This is a unique chance to ask a group of NVMe experts all your disk/NVMe questions.

To pique your interest, take another look at these two papers:

  1. [What Modern NVMe Storage Can Do, And How To Exploit It: High-Performance I/O for High-Performance Storage Engines](https://vldb.org/pvldb/vol16/p2090-haas.pdf)
  2. [I/O Interface Independence with xNVMe](https://dl.acm.org/doi/pdf/10.1145/3534056.3534936)

Authors of 2., Klaus and Simon, will be present for the AMA.

### Where?

Find it [here](https://www.reddit.com/r/databasedevelopment/comments/1afpezz/samsung_nvme_developers_ama/)!

### When?

January 31st, 2024 at 2PM EST / 7PM UTC.

### Who?

Klaus is maintainer of the QEMU emulated NVMe device and coauthor on a number of ratified NVMe Technical Proposals.
