title = Best of the New York Tech Scene date = January 2025 ---

These are some of my favorite active events and groups in New York. This is an opinionated guide, not intended to list every event and group there is. But I've probably forgotten some good ones. And I'm always curious to [learn](mailto:phil@eatonphil.com) about new groups.

Talk serieses

  • [NYC Systems](https://nycsystems.xyz/)
  • [Papers We Love](https://paperswelove.org/chapter/newyork/)
  • [Engineering Leadership NYC](https://www.linkedin.com/company/engineering-leadership-nyc)

I haven't attended the following but they look promising:

  • [New York Systems Reading Group](https://notes.ekzhang.com/events/nysrg)
  • [New York Enterprise Tech Meetup](https://www.work-bench.com/new-york-enterprise-tech-meetup)
  • [New York Site Reliability Engineering Tech Talks](https://www.meetup.com/new-york-site-reliability-engineering-tech-talks/)
  • [-1 to Now](https://lu.ma/southparkcommons-events)

Happy hours

  • [infra.nyc](https://infra.nyc/)
  • [NYC Systems at Night](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/13q3JVO3oxWjyBH_BwGYRBq9bYUksU3ju0d73XjvUSRU/)
  • [Browsertech NYC](https://browsertech.com/nyc)
