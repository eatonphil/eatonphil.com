title = Software Internals Book Club ---

I run a little email book club reading through books on software internals. We've had about 300-600 folk join us for each reading so far.

Sign up [here](https://forms.gle/xbBRN2Q2UFK9muXN6) to stay in the loop.

Loose criteria

The focus on is on books that would be interesting and useful to senior+ developers (whatever that means to you), though anyone is welcome.

  • Between 350-550 pages
  • Focused on a specific software topic, not on philosophy of software in general
  • Generally not textbooks
  • Completable in around 3 months reading 1-2 chapters per week
