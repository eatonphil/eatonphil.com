title = Let's chat! ---

I absolutely love meeting people. And I go out of my way to make spend 30 minutes with any person who donates at least $100 to one of the following non-profits and sends me a receipt. The donation can be dedicated to "Chat for Education".

  • [Malala Fund](https://malala.org/donate) ([100% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/811397590))
  • [ProLiteracy](https://www.proliteracy.org/donate/) ([100% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/166076384))
  • [UNICEF](https://www.unicefusa.org/how-help/ways-give?form=FUNSHMMBCHY) ([94% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/131760110))
  • [Girls Who Code](https://give.girlswhocode.com/give/77372/#!/donation/checkout) ([100% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/166076384))
  • [Scholarship America](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/042296967) ([99% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/042296967))

I've raised a humble $6,925 for these groups since starting this program in November 2024. But you set something like this up and keep doing it over time and who knows where it'll be in a decade!

If you cannot donate the $100 minimum to one of these non-profits, I would still be happy to hear from you! Just no guarantees on timing or availability.

Please [send me an email](mailto:phil@eatonphil.com) with the following to help me out:

  • Your name
  • Your LinkedIn
  • What are we talking about?
  • In-person or remote?
  • If in-person, when will you be in NYC?
  • If remote, what is your timezone and best hours?
