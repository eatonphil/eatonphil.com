title = Sundays Reading in the Park, NYC ---

One of my favorite things to do on a Sunday is to visit my favorite parks and read a book. And while I normally do this alone, I'd kind of rather do it with other people!

Fill out [this Google Form](https://forms.gle/XnHZDdkfM56MmZKy8) if you'd like to get invites!

Who is this for?

If you love to read and you love parks, this is for you!

How will it work?

I will send out a request for invites each week it runs. I'll either run this once a month or every other week depending on interest and turnout. Like other events I run, the first 20 people to respond to a calendar invite! Please don't show up without a calendar invite!

The quiet reading session will run from 11AM-11:45AM. After that it's unstructured social time.

How often?

I go almost every weekend to a park to read. And the weather isn't perfect year round. So it would be great to run this every other weekend, but that will depend on interest/turnout.

What prompted this?

Most of my network is software developers and I'd like to escape the bubble. I keep running events like the [NYC Systems Coffee Club](https://eatonphil.com/nyc-systems-coffee-club.html) and [NYC Systems](https://nycsystems.xyz), so I've been wanting to try something completely unrelated to career networking.
