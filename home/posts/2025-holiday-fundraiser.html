title = 2025 Holiday Fundraiser ---

Access to (and support for) education is incredibly important to me. So I'm celebrating this holiday season with two of my favorite things: chatting with you and fundraising for educational non-profits.

If you donate any amount over $10 and [send me a receipt](mailto:phil@eatonphil.com) (screenshot is fine), I will list your name and a link of your choice (SFW, etc.) on this page.

If you donate any amount over $50 and [send me a receipt](mailto:phil@eatonphil.com) (screenshot is fine), I will take a 30-minute call with you on any topic you'd like. Or I'll meet you in person in NYC when you're around.

Any of these non-profits are game:

  • [Malala Fund](https://malala.org/donate) ([100% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/811397590))
  • [ProLiteracy](https://www.proliteracy.org/donate/) ([100% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/166076384))
  • [UNICEF](https://www.unicefusa.org/how-help/ways-give?form=FUNSHMMBCHY) ([94% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/131760110))
  • [Girls Who Code](https://give.girlswhocode.com/give/77372/#!/donation/checkout) ([100% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/166076384))
  • [Scholarship America](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/042296967) ([99% Charity Score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/042296967))

I will close down this fundraiser on January 1st. The existing [Chat for Education](/chat.html) fundraiser is always ongoing.
