### Where?

Bryant Park! During the cold seasons, 1 Bryant Park has an indoor public space on the ground floor we can hang out at.

### When?

Once a month 9AM for ~45 minutes. Day depends on availability. Stay long or short. We can fit about 20 people, so I'll send out a request for invites once a month and the first 20 to respond get in!

### Who?

If you're a developer working on or are interested in any of the following topics, you should come! (Not a complete list.)

  • Compilers / Virtual Machines
  • Databases / Storage
  • Distributed Systems
  • Web Browsers
  • Operating Systems / Linux Kernel
  • Formal Methods / Verification / Testing
  • High Frequency Trading
  • High Performance Computing

We're all about software internals.
