title = Chat for Education Fundraiser ---

Hi! I'm Phil (he/him). I've been a developer, engineering manager, and a cofounder. I run a popular [Twitter](https://twitter.com/eatonphil) account. I run the [Software Internals Discord](https://eatonphil.com/discord.html), the [Software Internals Book Club](https://eatonphil.com/bookclub.html), the [NYC Systems Coffee Club](https://eatonphil.com/nyc-systems-coffee-club.html), and I co-organize [NYC Systems](https://nycsystems.xyz/).

I chat with hundreds of people (students, developers, managers, founders, folks in VC, etc.) each year over coffee just because I enjoy meeting people.

But during the month of November I'd like to put that time to use on something even more broadly impactful.

During the month of November I will be accepting invitations for a 30-minute chat when you make a donation of at least $200 to [Scholarship America](https://scholarshipamerica.org). Scholarship America is a [four-star charity with a 99% score](https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/042296967) according to Charity Navigator.

How to qualify

Make a donation of at least $200 to Scholarship America [here](https://scholarshipamerica.org/donate/) and [email me](mailto:phil@eatonphil.com) the receipt.

In the Dedication section feel free to use "Chat for Education" as the First Name and leave the Last Name field blank.

When you email me, please include your availability, your LinkedIn, your Twitter handle (if you will allow me to publicly thank you), and what you'd like to talk about!

I will make myself available between the hours of 7AM and 8PM NY time.

Suggested topics

Preface: I am very careful about saying what (I think) I am good at. Moreover I hope you see this fundementally as an excuse to donate to an education charity more than anything else. But in any case, it seemed appropriate to be up front about what I'm giving you in return!

I am an expert on technical writing, technical marketing, and building technical communities. In my actual day job I am a programmer building databases and distributed systems. But I am not an *expert* on those topics.

Any of these would probably make a good topic! But do not feel limited by these either.

If none of these topics nor any other aspect of my background resonates with you, this effort probably isn't a good fit for you and that's ok!

Matching donations

If you or your company is interested in matching donations, please [email me](mailto:phil@eatonphil.com) your information including your name and logo and the amount up to which you will match. At the end of the month I will submit all the receipts I have received to you as proof.

Your name and logo will be displayed on this page and I will be free to mention your participation on social media.
