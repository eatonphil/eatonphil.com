At the moment, WeWork doesn't give you a catalog of locations and amenities. Which ones have espresso machines? All access rooms? Who knows!

So I decided to visit every WeWork in Manhattan south of Central Park. All 28 of them.

(Not actually that many. Many that show up on Google Maps are not actually around anymore.)
