### Basics

Of Raft:

  • [In Search of an Understandable Consensus Algorithm](https://raft.github.io/raft.pdf) by Diego Ongaro and John Ousterhout
  • [Raft TLA+ spec](https://github.com/ongardie/raft.tla/blob/master/raft.tla) by Diego Ongaro
  • [Consensus: Bridging Theory and Practice](https://web.stanford.edu/~ouster/cgi-bin/papers/OngaroPhD.pdf) by Diego Ongaro (extended version of Raft paper)

Of Viewstamped Replication:

  • [Viewstamped Replication Revisited](https://pmg.csail.mit.edu/papers/vr-revisited.pdf) by Barbara Liskov and James Cowling
  • [Viewstamped Replication Revisited TLA+ spec](https://github.com/Vanlightly/vsr-tlaplus/blob/main/vsr-revisited/paper/VSR.tla) by Jack Vanlightly
