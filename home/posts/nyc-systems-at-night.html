title = NYC Systems at Night ---

This is a pay-your-own-way happy hour for folks in databases, compilers, security, infrastructure, and so on.

We meet at a bar in the last week of the month. We can fit about 30 people, so I'll send out a request for invites once a month and the first 30 to respond get in!

Join 100 other systems nerds in NYC: put your name and email down [here](https://forms.gle/4vHXtdhSx8iSJZqb6)!

### Who?

If you're a developer, manager, founder, investor working on or are interested in any of the following topics, you should come! (Not a complete list.)

  • Compilers / Virtual Machines
  • Databases / Storage
  • Distributed Systems
  • Web Browsers
  • Operating Systems / Linux Kernel
  • Formal Methods / Verification / Testing
  • High Frequency Trading
  • High Performance Computing

We're all about software internals.
