title = Phil Eaton ---

Hey! I'm Phil (he/him). In the past I've been a developer, a manager, and a cofounder. At companies as large as Oracle and as small as TigerBeetle. Today I'm a staff engineer working on Postgres products at [EnterpriseDB](https://www.enterprisedb.com/blog/importance-of-giving-back-to-postgresql).

I am a college dropout (not by choice; stay in school!) and for the last 10 years I've chased my way down the software stack starting from humble beginnings with the venerable jQuery and PHP.

I try to practice [healthy obsession](https://notes.eatonphil.com/2024-08-24-obsession.html).

To that end, I run the [NYC Systems Coffee Club](https://eatonphil.com/nyc-systems-coffee-club.html) (400+ members), co-run the [NYC Systems](https://nycsystems.xyz) talk series (1.2k+ members), run an online [Software Internals Book Club](https://eatonphil.com/bookclub.html) (1.5k+ members), run [/r/databasedevelopment](https://www.reddit.com/r/databasedevelopment/) (8.5k+ members), and I run a [Software Internals Discord](https://eatonphil.com/discord.html) (7.3k+ members) that has, among other things, become a home for database and distributed systems developers.

I play a good deal of [volleyball](https://notes.eatonphil.com/2024-12-26-volleyball-in-nyc.html) in NYC, keep trying to [cook new dishes](https://www.instagram.com/eatonphilz), and love to [read](https://www.goodreads.com/eatonphil).

My [writing](https://notes.eatonphil.com), which in 2024 [passed](https://notes.eatonphil.com/2024-11-28-1-million-views.html) 1 million total views, has been cited in the book [Writing for Developers](https://www.manning.com/books/writing-for-developers).

I hope you connect with me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/eatonphil/) (8.4k+ followers), [Twitter](https://x.com/eatonphil) (23k+ followers), [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/eatonphil.bsky.social) (4.8k+ followers), my [mailing list](https://eatonphil.com/subscribe.html) (3k+ subscribers), or over [email](mailto:phil@eatonphil.com).
