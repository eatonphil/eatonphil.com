title = Phil Eaton ---

Hey! I'm Phil (he/him). In the past I've been a developer, a manager, and a cofounder. Today I'm a staff engineer working on Postgres products at [EnterpriseDB](https://www.enterprisedb.com/blog/importance-of-giving-back-to-postgresql).

I am a college dropout (not by choice; stay in school!) and for the last 10 years I've chased my way down the software stack starting from humble beginnings with the venerable jQuery and PHP.

I run the [NYC Systems Coffee Club](https://eatonphil.com/nyc-systems-coffee-club.html), co-run the [NYC Systems](https://nycsystems.xyz) talk series, run an online [Software Internals Book Club](https://eatonphil.com/bookclub.html), and I run a [Software Internals Discord](https://eatonphil.com/discord.html) that has in particular become a home for database and distributed systems developers.

I absolutely love to meet people. If you ever want to catch up, [here's a little page](/chat.html) to help me out with scheduling.
