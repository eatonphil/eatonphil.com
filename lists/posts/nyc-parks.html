title = Favorite parks in NYC ---

Grand Central, Hudson River Park, The Highline, and Prospect Park go without saying.

Major parks

  • Fort Tryon Park (Hudson Heights, Manhattan)
  • Brooklyn Bridge Park (Brooklyn Heights, Brooklyn)
  • Bronx Forest (Bronx Zoo, Bronx)
  • Little Bay Park (Bayside, Queens)
  • Shore Road Promenade (Bay Ridge, Brooklyn)
  • Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge (Jamaica, Queens)
  • Highland Park (Cypress Hills, Brooklyn/Ridgewood, Queens)

There are a few other major ones I have not been to, but plan to: Van Cortlandt, Pelham Bay, Inwood Hill, Shirley Chisholm, Jacob Riis, Freshkills, etc.

Parks with potential

These parks are somewhat rundown but have great potential if they were better funded.

  • Flushing Meadow Corona Park

    Notes: The area around the lake floods constantly and has extremely few bathrooms. It's more fun during the summer when it's dry and you can watch 10s of soccer games going on, tons of cricket games too. Do the fountains at the Unisphere or the Fountain of the Planets ever work?

  • Flushing Bay Promenade

    Notes: I guess it's a part of Flushing Meadow Corona Park. Similar to FMCP, it does not seem to get enough love (funding).

  • Forest Park

    Notes: Overrated and unfortunately feels slightly rundown. Still nice to visit during the day but would be great if they could remove or replace all the broken light fixtures throughout the park.
