title = Software resources not to forget about date = Last updated September 9, 2023 ---

This isn't meant to be a big list. The smaller the list, the more meaningful it is. That said, I'd love to hear from you about what is or would be on your list.

### Architecture
  • [Simon Eskildsen's Napkin math numbers](https://github.com/sirupsen/napkin-math)
  • Designing Data Intensive Applications
  • High Performance Browser Networking
  • [Jepsen consistency models](https://jepsen.io/consistency)
  • [Martin Kleppmann's Transaction isolation level tests across major databases](https://github.com/ept/hermitage)
### Testing
  • [Andrey Satarin's collection on testing distributed systems](https://github.com/asatarin/testing-distributed-systems)
### Benchmarking
  • [Fair Benchmarking Considered Difficult: Common Pitfalls In Database Performance Testing](https://mytherin.github.io/papers/2018-dbtest.pdf)
