title = Do I need to move to NYC or SF for good opportunities in tech? date = October 12, 2023 ---

This is a great question. And aside from purely personal reasons (family, apartment size, etc.), there are a few different things to consider.

Geographical hiring market tiers

Your average company in your average US city builds a line-of-business app (a CRUD app). Salary probably peaks around $120k for a software developer. You mostly likely don't get any equity, but you may get a regular year-end bonus (possibly from a profit-sharing agreement).

Most people at the company stay at the company for 5-10 years. They're interested in tech and probably read Hacker News and /r/programming. They probably own an Arduino. But they aren't particularly obsessed with tech and they enjoy their home life, kids, and community.

This is amazing and nothing wrong whatsoever.
